Fertilizer Guide

Learn about different types of fertilizers and their optimal usage for better crop yield.

Urea

Nitrogen (N): 46%

Best for leafy vegetables and promoting vegetative growth.

Usage Guidelines

  • Apply 2-3 weeks before planting
  • Keep away from plant stems
  • Water thoroughly after application

Triple Superphosphate

Phosphorus (P): 46%

Excellent for root development and flowering.

Usage Guidelines

  • Mix with soil before planting
  • Apply near root zone
  • Ideal for acidic soils

Potassium Chloride

Potassium (K): 60%

Improves fruit quality and disease resistance.

Usage Guidelines

  • Apply during fruiting stage
  • Avoid in saline soils
  • Water well after application

Compost

Organic Matter: Rich in NPK

Natural soil enrichment and sustainable farming.

Usage Guidelines

  • Mix with soil before planting
  • Use as mulch
  • Apply every growing season

Best Practices

Timing

Apply fertilizers early morning or late evening for best results.

Moisture

Ensure proper soil moisture before applying fertilizers.

Quantity

Follow recommended dosage to avoid over-fertilization.
