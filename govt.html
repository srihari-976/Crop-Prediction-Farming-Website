Government Agricultural Schemes

Explore various government initiatives and schemes designed to support farmers and agriculture.

PM-KISAN

Direct income support of ₹6,000 per year to eligible farmer families.

Key Benefits

  • Direct cash transfer to farmer's account
  • No intermediaries involved
  • Three installments of ₹2,000 each
Learn More

PM Fasal Bima Yojana

Comprehensive crop insurance scheme to protect against crop loss.

Key Benefits

  • Coverage for all food crops
  • Low premium rates
  • Quick claim settlement
Learn More

Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization

Support for purchase of agricultural machinery and equipment.

Key Benefits

  • Subsidies on equipment purchase
  • Training and demonstration
  • Custom hiring facilities
Learn More

E-NAM

Electronic trading platform for agricultural commodities.

Key Benefits
